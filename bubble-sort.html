Bubble Sort Visualizer

Overview

Bubble Sort is a simple comparison-based sorting algorithm. It repeatedly steps through the list, compares adjacent elements, and swaps them if they are in the wrong order. Each pass moves the largest unsorted element to its correct place.

Complexity: Worst-case O(n²), Best-case O(n) (with early stop). Stable and in-place.

Pseudocode

for i = 0 to n - 2
  for j = 0 to n - i - 2
    if arr[j] > arr[j + 1]
      swap(arr[j], arr[j + 1])

Process: After each outer loop, the largest element among the unsorted portion ends at the right side (like bubbles rising).
